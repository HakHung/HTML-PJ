Accomodation


One World Hotel

Directly linked to the six-storey 1 Utama, One World Hotel is a five-star property that impresses from the get-go, with its facade of gleaming golden marble, striking chandeliers and sweeping stairways.Hotel facilities are impressive with a pool with a waterslide for kids, a gym with personal trainers and scheduled yoga classes, four tennis courts and a spa offering a 20% discount to in-house guests.


Hilton Garden Inn

Situated in Kuala Lumpur, 2.2 km from Putra World Trade Centre, Hilton Garden Inn Kuala Lumpur - South features air-conditioned accommodation and an outdoor swimming pool. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Private parking is available on site.


Sunway Hotel Resort & Spa

Blending comfort with a sense of exclusivity, Sunway Hotel Resort & Spa has 441 guestrooms and suites together with four F&B outlets and no less than 55 meeting rooms, all under its roof.Right next door is the one-stop destination for fun, Sunway Lagoon theme park, and the family-oriented shopping mall, Sunway Pyramid, interconnected with a covered walkway within the hotel
