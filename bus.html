Rapid KL is a public transportation system built by Prasarana Malaysia and operated by its subsidiaries, covering the Kuala Lumpur and Klang Valley areas. Rapid KL is one of the components of the Klang Valley Integrated Transit System. The acronym stands for Rangkaian Pengangkutan Intergrasi Deras Kuala Lumpur.




Smart Selangor, also known as Selangor Smart Bus, is a public transportation provider in Kuala Lumpur that operates Bus routes since 2015.The Smart Selangor has 46 Bus routes in Kuala Lumpur with 1150 Bus stops.Their Bus routes cover an area from the Sek Asrama Penuh Intergasi Sb stop to the Pusat Komersial Bandar Bukit Mahkota stop and from the Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Mawar stop to the Taman Sepang Putra, Tanjung Sepat stop.
